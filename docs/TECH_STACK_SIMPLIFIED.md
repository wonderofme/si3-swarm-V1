## How Does Kaia Work?

### The Basics

Kaia is built using **artificial intelligence** (AI) technology that allows her to:
- Understand what you're asking
- Remember your preferences and profile
- Find other members who share your interests
- Answer questions about Web3 topics
- Communicate in multiple languages

### The Technology Behind Kaia

**1. AI Brain (OpenAI GPT-4o-mini)**
- This is Kaia's "thinking" system
- It helps her understand your messages and respond naturally
- It's like having a smart assistant that learns from conversations

**2. Memory System (PostgreSQL Database)**
- Kaia remembers your profile, interests, and match history
- All your information is stored securely
- This allows Kaia to provide personalized matches and responses

**3. Communication Platform (Telegram)**
- Kaia lives on Telegram, a messaging app
- You chat with her just like texting a friend
- She's available 24/7 to help you

**4. Matching Engine**
- Kaia uses your interests and goals to find compatible members
- She looks for people with similar interests, roles, or goals
- Matches are based on what you've shared in your profile
